My Projects

Handwritten Japanese Hiragana recognition using a deep convolutional neural network. My model follows the framework of VGGNet and it performs over 98% accuracy on teh test set. This project is inspired by this thesis My model outperms the score presented in this thesis by around 2-3% in test accuracy thanks to data augmentation and other factors.
